Monochat Rules


  • 1. No Illegal Links: Do not share links to illegal content such as phishing, malware, pirated material, or anything that violates the law.

    2. Freedom of Speech: You have the freedom to speak your mind, but don’t promote illegal activity or harm.

    3. No Doxxing: Never share anyone’s personal information (including yours) without consent.

    4. Custom Rooms: Create any room, but keep content within legal boundaries. Mods may lock or delete rooms if necessary.

    5. No Spam: Avoid flooding the chat with repeated or irrelevant messages/links.

    6. Minimal Moderation: Moderators only intervene for illegal content or severe disruptions.

    7. Stay Secure: Respect others’ privacy and security. Report suspicious links.
